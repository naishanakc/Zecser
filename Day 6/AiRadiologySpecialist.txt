Job Summary 
An AI Radiology Specialist develops and applies artificial intelligence models to assist in 
detecting diseases from medical images (X-rays, CT, MRI). 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop/train AI models for image analysis (e.g., tumor detection)  
• Integrate AI tools into radiology workflows  
• Validate AI outputs with radiologists  
• Work on automation of reporting systems  
• Collaborate with doctors, engineers, and data scientists  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc in Radiology, Computer Science, or Biomedical Engineering  
• OR MBBS + interest in AI  
• Skills in Python, Machine Learning, Deep Learning  
Skills Required 
• Machine learning (CNNs, deep learning)  
• Medical imaging knowledge  
• Programming (Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch)  
• Data analysis  
Work Settings 
• Health-tech companies  
• AI startups  
• Hospitals with AI integration  
• Research labs  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹6–12 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹12–30 LPA  
• Senior: ₹30–80+ LPA  
Career Growth 
AI Specialist → AI Lead → Healthcare AI Architect → CTO (HealthTech)
