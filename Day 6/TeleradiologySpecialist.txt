Job Summary 
A Teleradiology Specialist supports remote radiology services, managing digital imaging 
workflows and assisting radiologists. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Coordinate remote image transfer and reporting  
• Manage PACS/RIS systems for remote access  
• Ensure timely delivery of reports  
• Maintain communication between hospitals and radiologists  
• Handle quality checks  
Qualification 
• BSc in Radiology / IT  
• Knowledge of PACS/RIS  
Skills 
• Digital workflow management  
• Communication skills  
• Technical troubleshooting  
Work Settings 
• Teleradiology companies  
• Remote healthcare services  
Salary 
₹4–10 LPA 
Career Growth 
Teleradiology Specialist → Operations Manager → Remote Imaging Director
