Job Summary 
An MRI Technician performs imaging using Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) to 
visualize soft tissues, brain, and organs. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Operate MRI machines  
• Screen patients for metal implants (safety check)  
• Monitor patients during scanning  
• Ensure image quality and accuracy  
• Follow strict safety protocols  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology  
• MRI training certification  
Skills 
• Knowledge of MRI physics and safety  
• Attention to detail  
• Patient care skills  
Work Settings 
• Advanced hospitals  
• Diagnostic imaging centers  
Salary 
₹4–10 LPA 
Career Growth 
MRI Technician → Senior MRI Specialist → Imaging Expert
