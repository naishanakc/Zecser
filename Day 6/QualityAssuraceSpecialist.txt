Job Summary 
A Radiology Quality Assurance (QA) Specialist ensures that imaging services meet quality 
standards, delivering accurate and consistent diagnostic results. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop and implement quality assurance programs  
• Monitor imaging quality and reporting accuracy  
• Conduct audits and performance evaluations  
• Ensure compliance with accreditation standards (e.g., NABH, JCI)  
• Identify errors and recommend improvements  
• Train staff on quality protocols  
• Maintain quality documentation and reports  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc in Radiology / Healthcare Quality / Medical Imaging  
• Certification in quality management (preferred)  
Skills 
• Quality control and auditing  
• Data analysis  
• Attention to detail  
• Process improvement  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
• Healthcare accreditation organizations  
Salary 
₹5–15 LPA 
Career Growth 
QA Specialist → Senior QA Manager → Quality Head → Accreditation Consultant
