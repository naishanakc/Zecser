Job Summary 
An MRI/CT Service Engineer specializes in installation, repair, and maintenance of high
end imaging systems, especially MRI and CT scanners. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Install MRI and CT machines  
• Diagnose and fix technical issues  
• Perform preventive maintenance  
• Calibrate and test system performance  
• Provide technical support to hospitals  
• Ensure minimal downtime of machines  
Qualification 
• BTech in Electronics / Biomedical Engineering  
• Specialized training in MRI/CT systems  
Skills 
• Advanced electronics knowledge  
• Troubleshooting complex systems  
• Field service skills  
• Time management  
Work Settings 
• Medical equipment companies (OEMs)  
• Hospitals (in-house engineers)  
• Service providers  
Salary 
₹5–15 LPA (can go higher with OEM experience) 
Career Growth 
Service Engineer → Senior Engineer → Regional Service Manager → Technical Director
