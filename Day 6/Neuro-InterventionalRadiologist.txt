Job Summary 
A Neuro-Interventional Radiologist treats brain and spinal vascular conditions using 
advanced catheter-based procedures. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform stroke interventions (mechanical thrombectomy)  
• Treat brain aneurysms and AVMs  
• Conduct cerebral angiography  
• Manage emergency neurovascular cases  
• Work closely with neurologists and neurosurgeons  
Qualification 
• MBBS  
• MD/DNB Radiology  
• Fellowship/DM in Neuro-Intervention  
Skills 
• Advanced neurovascular knowledge  
• High precision and speed (life-saving procedures)  
• Strong stress management  
Work Settings 
• Stroke centers  
• Neuro-specialty hospitals  
• Tertiary care hospitals  
Salary 
₹40 LPA – ₹1.5 Cr+ 
Work Nature 
• Highly critical, emergency-driven role  
• Life-saving procedures with high responsibility
