Job Summary 
An Imaging Informatics Specialist bridges radiology and IT, optimizing imaging systems, 
workflows, and data integration. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and manage imaging IT systems  
• Integrate PACS, RIS, and hospital systems  
• Improve workflow efficiency  
• Ensure data security and compliance  
• Support digital transformation in radiology  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc in Health Informatics / IT / Radiology  
• Certifications in healthcare IT (preferred)  
Skills 
• Health informatics  
• System integration  
• Data security & compliance  
Work Settings 
• Large hospitals  
• Health-tech companies  
• Government health systems  
Salary 
₹8–25+ LPA
