 Patient Care Coordinator (Radiology 
Department) 
Job Summary 
A Patient Care Coordinator manages patient interactions, scheduling, and overall 
experience within the radiology department. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Schedule imaging appointments  
• Coordinate between patients, doctors, and technicians  
• Explain procedures and billing details  
• Manage patient queries and complaints  
• Ensure smooth workflow and minimal waiting time  
• Maintain patient records and follow-ups  
Qualification 
• Bachelor’s degree in Healthcare Administration / Any field  
• Experience in hospital administration (preferred)  
Skills 
• Communication and interpersonal skills  
• Organizational and multitasking ability  
• Customer service orientation  
• Basic healthcare knowledge  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
• Radiology departments  
Salary 
₹2.5–6 LPA 
Career Growth 
Coordinator → Senior Coordinator → Operations Manager → Hospital Administrator
