Job Summary 
A General Radiologist is a medical doctor who interprets a wide range of imaging studies (X
ray, CT, MRI, Ultrasound) to diagnose diseases and guide treatment across multiple organ 
systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Interpret imaging scans (X-rays, CT, MRI, Ultrasound)  
• Diagnose conditions such as fractures, tumors, infections, and organ abnormalities  
• Provide written diagnostic reports to referring physicians  
• Consult with clinicians regarding patient cases  
• Ensure radiation safety and imaging protocols  
• Supervise radiology technicians and imaging procedures  
Qualification & Education 
• MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)  
• MD/DNB in Radiology  
• Medical Council Registration (NMC/State Council in India)  
Skills Required 
• Strong diagnostic and analytical skills  
• Knowledge of anatomy and pathology  
• Attention to detail  
• Clinical decision-making ability  
• Communication with doctors and patients  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals (Government & Private)  
• Diagnostic imaging centers  
• Multispecialty clinics  
Salary (India Estimate) 
• Entry Level: ₹8–15 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹15–30 LPA  
• Senior: ₹30–60+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Junior Radiologist → Consultant Radiologist → Senior Consultant → Head of Department 
(HOD)
