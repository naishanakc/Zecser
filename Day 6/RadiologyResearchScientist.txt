Job Summary 
A Radiology Research Scientist conducts advanced research in medical imaging 
technologies, improving diagnostic techniques, imaging systems, and AI applications. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Design and conduct research studies in radiology  
• Develop new imaging techniques and protocols  
• Work on AI/ML models for medical imaging  
• Publish research papers in scientific journals  
• Collaborate with doctors, engineers, and institutions  
• Apply for research grants and funding  
Qualification 
• PhD in Radiology / Biomedical Engineering / Medical Imaging  
• OR MD Radiology with strong research experience  
Skills Required 
• Research methodology  
• Data analysis and statistics  
• Scientific writing  
• Innovation and problem-solving  
Work Settings 
• Research institutes  
• Universities  
• Healthcare R&D companies  
• Government research organizations  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹6–12 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹12–25 LPA  
• Senior: ₹25–50+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Research Scientist → Senior Scientist → Principal Investigator → Research Director
