Job Summary 
A Cruise Ship Radiologist provides onboard diagnostic imaging services for passengers 
and crew during voyages. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform and interpret basic imaging (X-ray, ultrasound)  
• Manage medical emergencies onboard  
• Provide immediate diagnostic support  
• Maintain onboard medical equipment  
• Work as part of ship medical team  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Experience in emergency care (preferred)  
• Certifications for maritime/ship medicine (optional)  
Skills 
• Emergency handling  
• Adaptability in isolated environments  
• Multitasking  
• Strong clinical judgment  
Work Settings 
• Cruise ships  
• Maritime healthcare services  
Salary 
₹30 LPA – ₹80+ LPA (plus travel & accommodation benefits) 
Advantages 
• Travel the world 🌍  
• Unique work environment  
• Tax-free income (in some cases)  
Work Nature 
• Limited resources onboard  
• Long contracts (months at sea)  
Career Growth 
Cruise Radiologist → Senior Ship Doctor → Maritime Medical Director
