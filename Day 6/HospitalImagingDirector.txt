Job Summary 
A Hospital Imaging Director is a senior leadership role responsible for strategic planning, 
management, and growth of all imaging services within a hospital or healthcare system. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Lead entire imaging services (radiology, nuclear medicine, interventional units)  
• Develop long-term strategy and expansion plans  
• Manage budgets, investments, and profitability  
• Ensure regulatory compliance and accreditation standards  
• Oversee staff recruitment, training, and performance  
• Implement new technologies (AI, advanced imaging systems)  
• Represent imaging services in executive leadership meetings  
Qualification 
• MD Radiology / OR BSc + MBA (with extensive experience)  
• Leadership experience in healthcare management  
Skills 
• Strategic leadership  
• Financial management  
• Decision-making  
• Healthcare policy understanding  
Work Settings 
• Large hospitals  
• Corporate hospital chains  
• Healthcare groups  
Salary 
₹25 LPA – ₹1 Cr+ 
Career Growth 
Imaging Director → Group Director → Chief Medical/Operations Officer
