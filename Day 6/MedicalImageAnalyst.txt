Job Summary 
A Medical Image Analyst focuses on processing and analyzing medical images for 
diagnosis, AI training, and research. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Annotate and label medical images  
• Analyze CT, MRI, and X-ray datasets  
• Support AI model training  
• Improve image quality and segmentation  
• Work with radiologists for validation  
Qualification 
• BSc in Radiology / Biomedical Engineering / Computer Science  
• Knowledge of image processing tools  
Skills 
• Image analysis  
• Annotation tools (e.g., labeling software)  
• Basic programming (Python/OpenCV)  
Work Settings 
• AI companies  
• Medical research labs  
• Imaging software companies  
Salary 
₹4–12 LPA 
Career Growth 
Image Analyst → Senior Analyst → AI Imaging Specialist
