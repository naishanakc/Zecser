Job Summary 
A Radiologic Technologist is a more advanced and trained imaging professional who 
performs multiple imaging modalities and ensures high-quality diagnostic imaging. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Operate X-ray, CT, MRI, and other imaging systems  
• Ensure high-quality imaging output  
• Assist in advanced diagnostic procedures  
• Maintain equipment and quality standards  
• Train junior technicians  
• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards  
Qualification 
• BSc in Radiologic Technology / Medical Imaging Technology  
Skills 
• Multi-modality expertise  
• Technical problem-solving  
• Knowledge of imaging protocols  
• Quality assurance  
Work Settings 
• Multispecialty hospitals  
• Advanced imaging centers  
Salary 
₹3–8 LPA (can go higher with specialization) 
Career Growth 
Technologist → Senior Technologist → Imaging Specialist → Manager
