Breast Radiologist (Mammography Specialist) 
Job Summary 
A Breast Radiologist focuses on breast imaging for early detection and diagnosis of breast 
cancer. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform and interpret mammography, ultrasound, breast MRI  
• Detect early-stage breast cancer  
• Conduct image-guided biopsies  
• Work closely with oncologists and surgeons  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Fellowship in Breast Imaging  
Skills 
• High attention to detail  
• Early cancer detection expertise  
• Patient counseling sensitivity  
Work Settings 
• Cancer hospitals  
• Women’s health centers  
Salary 
₹20–60+ LPA
