Job Summary 
A Mammography Technician specializes in breast imaging for early detection of breast 
cancer. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform mammography procedures  
• Position patients for breast imaging  
• Ensure minimal discomfort during procedures  
• Maintain high image quality  
• Assist radiologists in cancer detection  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology  
• Certification in Mammography (preferred)  
Skills 
• Attention to detail  
• Patient sensitivity and communication  
• Knowledge of breast imaging techniques  
Work Settings 
• Cancer hospitals  
• Women’s health centers  
Salary 
₹3–8 LPA
