 Biomedical Engineer (Imaging 
Equipment) 
Job Summary 
A Biomedical Engineer (Imaging Equipment) designs, maintains, and improves medical 
imaging devices such as X-ray, CT, MRI, and ultrasound systems. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Install and maintain imaging equipment  
• Troubleshoot technical faults in machines  
• Ensure equipment complies with safety standards  
• Collaborate with doctors and technicians to optimize usage  
• Upgrade and calibrate systems  
• Maintain technical documentation  
Qualification 
• BTech/BSc in Biomedical Engineering / Electronics / Instrumentation  
Skills Required 
• Electronics and medical device knowledge  
• Problem-solving and troubleshooting  
• Understanding of imaging systems  
• Technical documentation skills  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Medical device companies  
• Diagnostic centers  
• Manufacturing companies  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹3–6 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹6–12 LPA  
• Senior: ₹12–25+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Biomedical Engineer → Senior Engineer → Technical Manager → Product Head
