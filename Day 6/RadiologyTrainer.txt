Job Summary 
A Radiology Trainer educates and trains radiology students, technicians, and healthcare 
professionals in imaging techniques, equipment usage, and best practices. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Conduct classroom and practical training sessions  
• Teach imaging modalities (X-ray, CT, MRI, Ultrasound)  
• Develop training materials and curriculum  
• Assess trainee performance and provide feedback  
• Train staff on new technologies and protocols  
• Ensure adherence to safety and quality standards  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc Radiology / MD Radiology (for advanced teaching)  
• Teaching certification (preferred)  
• Clinical or technical experience in radiology  
Skills Required 
• Teaching and presentation skills  
• Deep subject knowledge  
• Communication and mentoring ability  
• Patience and adaptability  
Work Settings 
• Medical colleges  
• Training institutes  
• Hospitals  
• EdTech platforms  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹4–8 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹8–15 LPA  
• Senior: ₹15–30+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Trainer → Senior Trainer → Academic Head → Education Director
