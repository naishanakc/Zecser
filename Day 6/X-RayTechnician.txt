Job Summary 
An X-Ray Technician specializes in performing X-ray imaging procedures to diagnose 
fractures, infections, and other conditions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform X-ray imaging procedures  
• Position patients correctly  
• Ensure radiation safety (lead aprons, shielding)  
• Process and maintain X-ray images  
• Assist doctors in diagnosis  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology  
Skills 
• Knowledge of X-ray techniques  
• Radiation protection  
• Patient handling  
Work Settings 
• Clinics  
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic labs  
Salary 
₹2–5 LPA 
Career Growth 
X-Ray Technician → Senior X-Ray Specialist → Radiology Technologist
