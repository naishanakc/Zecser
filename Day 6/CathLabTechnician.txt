Job Summary 
A Cath Lab Technician assists in cardiac catheterization and interventional procedures, 
helping diagnose and treat heart conditions. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assist cardiologists during angiography and angioplasty  
• Operate cath lab imaging equipment  
• Prepare patients for cardiac procedures  
• Monitor vital signs during procedures  
• Maintain sterile environment and equipment  
• Handle emergency situations in cardiac procedures  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology / Cardiovascular Technology  
• Cath lab training certification  
Skills 
• Knowledge of cardiac anatomy  
• Emergency response skills  
• Technical equipment handling  
• Team coordination  
Work Settings 
• Cardiac hospitals  
• Cath labs in multispecialty hospitals  
Salary 
₹4–10 LPA 
Career Growth 
Cath Lab Technician → Senior Cath Lab Specialist → Cardiac Technologist → Cath Lab 
Manager
