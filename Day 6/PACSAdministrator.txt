 PACS Administrator (Picture Archiving 
and Communication System) 
Job Summary 
A PACS Administrator manages systems used to store, retrieve, and share medical images 
digitally within hospitals. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Maintain PACS servers and imaging databases  
• Ensure secure storage and backup of images  
• Troubleshoot system issues  
• Integrate PACS with hospital systems  
• Manage user access and data security  
Qualification 
• BSc in IT / Radiology / Computer Science  
• Training in PACS systems  
Skills 
• Networking and server management  
• Knowledge of DICOM standards  
• Problem-solving  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
• IT healthcare companies  
Salary 
₹5–18 LPA 
Career Growth 
PACS Admin → Senior Admin → Imaging IT Manager
