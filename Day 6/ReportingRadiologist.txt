Job Summary 
A Reporting Radiologist (Teleradiologist) remotely interprets medical images and provides 
diagnostic reports using digital systems, often serving hospitals across different locations or 
countries. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Analyze imaging studies remotely (CT, MRI, X-ray)  
• Generate and deliver structured reports digitally  
• Work with PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System)  
• Ensure quick turnaround time (TAT) for reports  
• Maintain diagnostic accuracy despite remote workflow  
• Communicate critical findings to referring doctors urgently  
Qualification & Education 
• MBBS  
• MD/DNB in Radiology  
• Training in PACS/RIS systems (preferred)  
Skills Required 
• Strong reporting speed and accuracy  
• Familiarity with digital radiology systems (PACS/RIS)  
• Ability to work independently  
• Time zone flexibility (night shifts common)  
• Strong written communication skills  
Work Settings 
• Teleradiology companies  
• Remote/home-based work  
• International reporting firms  
• Hospitals outsourcing radiology services  
Salary (India Estimate) 
• Entry Level: ₹12–20 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹25–50 LPA  
• Senior/International: ₹50 LPA – ₹1 Cr+
