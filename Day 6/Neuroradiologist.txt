 Neuroradiologist (Brain & Spine Imaging) 
Job Summary 
A Neuroradiologist specializes in imaging of the brain, spine, and nervous system, 
diagnosing conditions like stroke, tumors, and neurological disorders. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Interpret MRI & CT scans of brain and spine  
• Diagnose stroke, brain tumors, hemorrhage, multiple sclerosis  
• Perform/assist in neuro-interventional procedures (optional)  
• Work closely with neurologists and neurosurgeons  
• Provide urgent reports in emergency neurological cases  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Fellowship in Neuroradiology  
Skills 
• Deep knowledge of neuroanatomy  
• High precision in MRI interpretation  
• Emergency decision-making  
Work Settings 
• Neuro hospitals  
• Stroke centers  
• Academic institutes  
Salary (India) 
₹20–60+ LPA
