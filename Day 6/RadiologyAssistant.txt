Job Summary 
A Radiology Assistant supports radiologists and technologists by preparing patients, 
managing workflow, and assisting in imaging procedures. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare patients for imaging (explain procedures, consent, positioning)  
• Assist during X-ray, CT, MRI, and ultrasound procedures  
• Maintain patient records and imaging documentation  
• Ensure cleanliness and readiness of imaging rooms  
• Help in patient transport and scheduling  
• Support basic equipment setup  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology / Healthcare Assistance  
• Basic medical training preferred  
Skills Required 
• Patient handling and communication  
• Basic medical knowledge  
• Organizational skills  
• Teamwork  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
• Imaging departments  
Salary (India) 
₹1.8–4 LPA 
Career Growth 
Radiology Assistant → Senior Assistant → Radiology Technician
