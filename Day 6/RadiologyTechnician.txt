Job Summary 
A Radiology Technician assists in performing basic diagnostic imaging procedures such as 
X-rays and supports radiologists in patient imaging workflows. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Operate X-ray and basic imaging equipment  
• Prepare patients for imaging procedures  
• Position patients correctly for accurate imaging  
• Maintain imaging records and reports  
• Follow radiation safety protocols  
• Assist radiologists during procedures  
Qualification 
• Diploma / BSc in Radiology or Imaging Technology  
Skills Required 
• Basic knowledge of human anatomy  
• Equipment handling skills  
• Patient care and communication  
• Attention to detail  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
• Clinics  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹1.8–3.5 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹3.5–6 LPA  
• Senior: ₹6–10+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Radiology Technician → Senior Technician → Supervisor → Department Manager
