Job Summary 
A Freelance Radiology Consultant provides independent radiology services to multiple 
hospitals, clinics, or diagnostic centers on a contract basis. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Provide diagnostic reporting for multiple clients  
• Offer second opinions and expert consultations  
• Support hospitals lacking full-time radiologists  
• Manage flexible schedules and client relationships  
• Maintain professional standards and compliance  
Qualification 
• MBBS  
• MD/DNB Radiology  
• Strong experience (usually 3–5+ years)  
Skills 
• Independent practice capability  
• Networking and client management  
• Time management  
• Strong diagnostic expertise  
Work Settings 
• Self-employed / freelance  
• Contract-based hospital work  
• Remote + on-site mix  
Earnings 
₹20 LPA – ₹1 Cr+ (depends on workload & clients) 
Career Growth 
Freelancer → Senior Consultant → Own Radiology Practice / Firm
