Job Summary 
A Radiology Operations Manager focuses on optimizing workflows, productivity, and 
efficiency across radiology services in hospitals or networks. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Analyze and improve radiology workflows  
• Monitor performance metrics (TAT, patient volume, utilization)  
• Implement process improvements  
• Coordinate between departments (ER, OPD, ICU)  
• Ensure quality standards and compliance  
• Manage multiple imaging units or centers  
Qualification 
• BSc Radiology / Healthcare Administration  
• MBA in Operations / Hospital Management (preferred)  
Skills 
• Data analysis and process optimization  
• Strategic thinking  
• Leadership  
• Problem-solving  
Work Settings 
• Large hospitals  
• Healthcare chains  
• Corporate healthcare organizations  
Salary 
₹10–30+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Operations Manager → Regional Operations Head → COO (Healthcare)
