 Application Specialist (Radiology 
Machines) 
Job Summary 
An Application Specialist trains healthcare professionals on how to use radiology 
equipment effectively and ensures optimal clinical usage. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Train doctors and technicians on imaging systems  
• Demonstrate machine features and workflows  
• Optimize imaging protocols  
• Provide post-installation support  
• Assist in product demonstrations and sales  
• Gather user feedback for product improvement  
Qualification 
• BSc Radiology / Biomedical Engineering  
• Experience with imaging systems  
Skills 
• Communication and training skills  
• Clinical workflow understanding  
• Technical knowledge of machines  
• Presentation skills  
Work Settings 
• Medical device companies  
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
Salary 
₹6–18 LPA 
Career Growth 
Application Specialist → Senior Specialist → Product Manager → Regional Head
