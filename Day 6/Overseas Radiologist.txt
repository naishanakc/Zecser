 Overseas Radiologist (Middle East, UK, 
USA, etc.) 
Job Summary 
An Overseas Radiologist works in international healthcare systems, providing diagnostic 
imaging services under country-specific medical regulations. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Interpret imaging studies as per international standards  
• Follow country-specific clinical protocols  
• Work with multidisciplinary teams  
• Maintain compliance with licensing and accreditation  
• Deliver high-quality patient care  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Licensing exams (varies by country):  
o UK: PLAB / FRCR  
o USA: USMLE  
o Middle East: DHA / HAAD / MOH  
• Work experience (preferred)  
Skills 
• Adaptability to international standards  
• Strong clinical knowledge  
• Communication skills (English proficiency)  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals abroad  
• Government health systems  
• Private healthcare chains  
Salary (Approx.) 
• Middle East: ₹40 LPA – ₹1 Cr+  
• UK: ₹60 LPA – ₹1.5 Cr+  
• USA: ₹2 Cr – ₹5 Cr+  
Advantages 
• High salary  
• Better infrastructure  
• Global exposure  
Challenges 
• Licensing exams  
• Relocation and cultural adaptation  
Career Growth 
Consultant Radiologist → Senior Consultant → Department Head → International Expert
