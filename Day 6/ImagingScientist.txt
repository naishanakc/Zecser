Job Summary 
An Imaging Scientist develops and improves imaging technologies and algorithms, often 
working at the intersection of engineering, physics, and medicine. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Develop imaging software and algorithms  
• Improve image quality and processing techniques  
• Work on AI-based image analysis  
• Collaborate with radiologists and engineers  
• Conduct experimental studies and validation  
Qualification 
• MSc/PhD in Imaging Science / Biomedical Engineering / Physics  
Skills 
• Image processing (MATLAB, Python)  
• Machine learning  
• Signal processing  
• Analytical thinking  
Work Settings 
• Tech companies  
• Research labs  
• Medical imaging firms  
Salary 
₹8–30+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Imaging Scientist → Senior Scientist → R&D Lead → Innovation Director
