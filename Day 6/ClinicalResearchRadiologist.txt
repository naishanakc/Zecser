Job Summary 
A Clinical Research Radiologist participates in clinical trials and imaging-based research, 
helping evaluate new treatments, drugs, and diagnostic tools. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Interpret imaging data for clinical trials  
• Ensure imaging protocols meet study standards  
• Collaborate with pharma companies and CROs  
• Monitor patient imaging outcomes  
• Maintain compliance with regulatory guidelines  
• Publish and present research findings  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Training in clinical research (GCP certification preferred)  
Skills 
• Clinical trial knowledge  
• Data interpretation  
• Regulatory compliance  
• Analytical thinking  
Work Settings 
• Pharmaceutical companies  
• Clinical research organizations (CROs)  
• Academic hospitals  
Salary 
₹15–40+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Clinical Research Radiologist → Principal Investigator → Medical Director (Research)
