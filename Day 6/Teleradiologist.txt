Teleradiologist (Remote Reporting) 
Job Summary 
A Teleradiologist interprets medical images remotely and provides diagnostic reports to 
hospitals across different locations or countries. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Analyze X-rays, CT, MRI scans via digital systems  
• Generate accurate and timely diagnostic reports  
• Use PACS/RIS systems for remote access  
• Communicate critical findings to referring doctors  
• Maintain high reporting quality and turnaround time (TAT)  
• Work across different time zones (night shifts common)  
Qualification 
• MBBS  
• MD/DNB in Radiology  
• Experience in diagnostic reporting  
• Familiarity with PACS systems  
Skills Required 
• High-speed and accurate reporting  
• Independent decision-making  
• Strong written communication  
• Digital workflow proficiency  
Work Settings 
• Work-from-home  
• Teleradiology companies  
• International healthcare networks  
Salary (India & Global) 
• India: ₹12–50+ LPA  
• International: ₹50 LPA – ₹1.5 Cr+  
Advantages 
• Remote work flexibility  
• Global case exposure  
• Better work-life balance (depending on shifts)  
Career Growth 
Teleradiologist → Senior Consultant → Quality Auditor → International Specialist
