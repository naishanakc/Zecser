Job Summary 
An Imaging Compliance Officer ensures that all radiology operations adhere to legal, 
ethical, and regulatory standards, minimizing risk and ensuring patient safety. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Ensure compliance with healthcare laws and regulations  
• Monitor adherence to imaging protocols and policies  
• Conduct internal audits and inspections  
• Prepare for external regulatory inspections  
• Maintain documentation for compliance records  
• Investigate compliance issues and implement corrective actions  
• Train staff on regulatory requirements  
Qualification 
• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Healthcare Administration / Radiology / Law  
• Certification in healthcare compliance (preferred)  
Skills 
• Knowledge of healthcare regulations  
• Risk management  
• Documentation and auditing  
• Communication and training  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic imaging chains  
• Government health departments  
Salary 
₹6–20+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Compliance Officer → Senior Compliance Manager → Compliance Director → Healthcare 
Policy Expert
