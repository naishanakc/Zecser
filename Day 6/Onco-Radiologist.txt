 Onco-Radiologist (Cancer Imaging Specialist) 
Job Summary 
An Onco-Radiologist specializes in imaging for cancer detection, staging, and treatment 
monitoring, playing a crucial role in oncology care. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Interpret CT, MRI, PET-CT scans for cancer diagnosis  
• Detect tumors and assess spread (metastasis)  
• Monitor response to chemotherapy/radiation therapy  
• Participate in tumor boards (multidisciplinary meetings)  
• Guide biopsies and image-based interventions  
• Collaborate with oncologists and surgeons  
Qualification 
• MBBS  
• MD/DNB Radiology  
• Fellowship in Onco-Radiology / Cancer Imaging  
Skills 
• Expertise in tumor imaging  
• Strong analytical and pattern-recognition skills  
• Multidisciplinary collaboration  
• Attention to detail for early detection  
Work Settings 
• Cancer hospitals  
• Oncology centers  
• Research institutes  
Salary 
₹25–70+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Onco-Radiologist → Senior Cancer Imaging Specialist → Oncology Imaging Director
