Job Summary 
A PET Scan Technologist performs Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scans, mainly 
used in cancer detection, staging, and treatment monitoring. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare and administer radioactive tracers (radiopharmaceuticals)  
• Operate PET or PET-CT scanners  
• Monitor patients during scanning procedures  
• Ensure radiation safety protocols are strictly followed  
• Assist in detecting cancer spread and metabolic activity  
• Maintain imaging quality and accurate records  
Qualification 
• BSc in Radiology / Nuclear Medicine / Imaging Technology  
• Certification in PET/CT technology (preferred)  
Skills Required 
• Knowledge of nuclear medicine and radiation safety  
• Precision and attention to detail  
• Patient care and monitoring  
• Technical proficiency with PET systems  
Work Settings 
• Cancer hospitals  
• Nuclear medicine departments  
• Advanced diagnostic centers  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹4–8 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹8–15 LPA  
• Senior: ₹15–25+ LPA  
Career Growth 
PET Technologist → Senior PET Specialist → Nuclear Medicine Expert → Supervisor
