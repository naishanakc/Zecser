 Medical Physicist (Radiology) 
Job Summary 
A Medical Physicist ensures safe and effective use of radiation in medical imaging and 
therapy. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Calibrate imaging and radiation equipment  
• Ensure radiation safety standards  
• Optimize imaging quality with minimal radiation dose  
• Conduct quality assurance tests  
• Train staff on radiation safety  
• Support radiotherapy and diagnostic imaging  
Qualification 
• MSc in Medical Physics / Radiological Physics  
• Certification from regulatory bodies (e.g., AERB in India)  
Skills 
• Physics and radiation principles  
• Quality control and safety  
• Analytical and technical skills  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Cancer treatment centers  
• Research institutes  
Salary 
₹6–20+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Medical Physicist → Senior Physicist → Radiation Safety Officer → Chief Physicist
