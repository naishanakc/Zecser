Job Summary 
A Radiology Lecturer or Professor teaches medical students and postgraduate trainees 
while also practicing radiology and conducting research. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Teach MBBS and MD radiology students  
• Conduct lectures, practical sessions, and assessments  
• Supervise research projects and theses  
• Publish academic research  
• Perform diagnostic imaging in teaching hospitals  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Teaching experience (required for higher positions)  
Skills 
• Teaching and mentoring  
• Communication and presentation  
• Academic research skills  
Work Settings 
• Medical colleges  
• Teaching hospitals  
• Universities  
Salary (India) 
• Lecturer: ₹8–15 LPA  
• Associate Professor: ₹15–30 LPA  
• Professor: ₹30–60+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Lecturer → Assistant Professor → Associate Professor → Professor → HOD
