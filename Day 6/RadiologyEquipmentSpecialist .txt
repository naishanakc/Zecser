Job Summary 
A Radiology Equipment Specialist ensures efficient operation, maintenance, and 
optimization of all imaging systems in a healthcare facility. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Monitor performance of imaging equipment  
• Schedule preventive maintenance  
• Coordinate repairs and upgrades  
• Train staff on proper equipment usage  
• Ensure compliance with regulatory standards  
• Maintain equipment inventory and records  
Qualification 
• BSc/BTech in Biomedical Engineering / Radiology Technology  
Skills 
• Equipment management  
• Technical troubleshooting  
• Training and coordination  
• Quality assurance  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic imaging centers  
Salary 
₹4–10 LPA 
Career Growth 
Equipment Specialist → Senior Specialist → Technical Operations Manager
