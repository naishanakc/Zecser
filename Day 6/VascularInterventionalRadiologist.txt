Job Summary 
A Vascular Interventional Radiologist specializes in treating diseases of blood vessels using 
minimally invasive techniques. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Perform angioplasty and stenting procedures  
• Treat blocked arteries and veins  
• Manage conditions like peripheral artery disease, varicose veins  
• Conduct embolization for bleeding or tumors  
• Place vascular access devices (ports, catheters)  
Qualification 
• MBBS  
• MD/DNB Radiology  
• Fellowship in Vascular Interventional Radiology  
Skills 
• Deep understanding of vascular anatomy  
• Precision in catheter-based procedures  
• Ability to manage complications  
Work Settings 
• Cardiac hospitals  
• Vascular specialty centers  
• High-end diagnostic hospitals  
Salary 
₹30–80 LPA (can exceed ₹1 Cr with experience) 
Career Growth 
Vascular IR → Senior Specialist → Endovascular Expert → Department Head
