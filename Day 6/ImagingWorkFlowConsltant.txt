Job Summary 
An Imaging Workflow Consultant analyzes and optimizes radiology department processes, 
improving efficiency, turnaround time, and patient experience. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Evaluate radiology workflows (patient flow, reporting, scheduling)  
• Identify inefficiencies and bottlenecks  
• Recommend process improvements and automation  
• Implement best practices for workflow optimization  
• Train staff on improved systems  
• Monitor performance metrics (TAT, utilization rates)  
Qualification 
• BSc Radiology / Healthcare Administration  
• MBA in Operations / Hospital Management (preferred)  
• Experience in radiology operations  
Skills 
• Process optimization  
• Data analysis  
• Problem-solving  
• Strategic thinking  
Work Settings 
• Healthcare consulting firms  
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic chains  
Salary 
₹8–25+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Consultant → Senior Consultant → Healthcare Strategy Manager → Director
