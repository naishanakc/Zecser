Job Summary 
A Radiology Department Manager oversees the daily operations of a hospital’s radiology 
department, ensuring efficient workflow, quality imaging services, and staff coordination. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Manage day-to-day radiology department operations  
• Supervise radiologists, technicians, and support staff  
• Ensure timely imaging services and report delivery  
• Maintain compliance with safety and regulatory standards  
• Handle budgeting, inventory, and resource allocation  
• Improve workflow efficiency and patient experience  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc in Radiology / Healthcare Administration  
• MBA in Hospital Management (preferred)  
• Experience in radiology department operations  
Skills Required 
• Leadership and team management  
• Operational planning  
• Communication and coordination  
• Problem-solving  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
Salary (India) 
• Entry: ₹6–12 LPA  
• Mid-Level: ₹12–20 LPA  
• Senior: ₹20–35+ LPA  
Career Growth 
Department Manager → Senior Manager → Hospital Operations Head
