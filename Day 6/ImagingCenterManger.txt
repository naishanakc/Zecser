Job Summary 
An Imaging Center Manager is responsible for overall management of standalone 
diagnostic centers, including operations, staff, and business performance. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Oversee daily operations of imaging center  
• Manage staff, scheduling, and patient flow  
• Ensure profitability and cost control  
• Maintain equipment and service quality  
• Handle marketing and business development  
• Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations  
Qualification 
• BSc Radiology / Healthcare Management  
• MBA (preferred)  
Skills 
• Business management  
• Financial planning  
• Customer service  
• Leadership  
Work Settings 
• Diagnostic imaging centers  
• Private healthcare chains  
Salary 
₹8–25+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Center Manager → Regional Manager → Business Head
