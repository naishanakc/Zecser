Job Summary 
A Radiology Nurse provides clinical care to patients undergoing imaging procedures, 
ensuring safety, comfort, and proper medical support. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare patients for imaging procedures  
• Administer IV contrast agents  
• Monitor vital signs during procedures  
• Assist radiologists during imaging  
• Handle patient emergencies and reactions  
• Provide post-procedure care  
Qualification 
• BSc Nursing / GNM  
• Nursing license (State/NMC registration)  
Skills 
• Clinical and patient care skills  
• Emergency handling  
• IV line management  
• Communication  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic imaging departments  
Salary 
₹3–8 LPA 
Career Growth 
Radiology Nurse → Senior Nurse → Nurse Supervisor → Nurse Manager
