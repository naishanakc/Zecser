Job Summary 
A Radiology Data Analyst analyzes imaging data and operational data to improve 
diagnosis, efficiency, and hospital performance. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Analyze radiology reports and imaging datasets  
• Identify trends in disease patterns  
• Optimize workflow efficiency  
• Create dashboards and reports  
• Support research and clinical decision-making  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc in Data Science, Statistics, Radiology, or IT  
• Knowledge of SQL, Excel, Python  
Skills 
• Data analysis & visualization  
• Statistical analysis  
• Healthcare data understanding  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Healthcare analytics firms  
• Research organizations  
Salary 
₹5–15 LPA 
Career Growth 
Data Analyst → Senior Analyst → Healthcare Data Scientist
