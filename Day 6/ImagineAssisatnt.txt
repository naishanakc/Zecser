Job Summary 
An Imaging Assistant provides technical and operational support in diagnostic imaging 
departments, assisting technologists in ensuring efficient imaging workflows. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Assist in preparing imaging equipment  
• Support technologists during scans  
• Manage patient flow and scheduling  
• Maintain imaging records and reports  
• Ensure safety protocols are followed  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology or Healthcare  
Skills 
• Technical support skills  
• Time management  
• Communication and coordination  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Imaging centers  
Salary 
₹2–4.5 LPA 
Career Growth 
Imaging Assistant → Radiology Technologist → Senior Technologist
