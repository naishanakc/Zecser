Job Summary 
An Interventional Radiology Nurse assists in minimally invasive procedures, providing pre
, intra-, and post-procedure patient care. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Prepare patients for interventional procedures  
• Assist during procedures (angiography, biopsies, embolization)  
• Monitor vital signs and sedation  
• Maintain sterile environment  
• Handle emergency situations  
• Provide post-procedure recovery care  
Qualification 
• BSc Nursing / GNM  
• Specialized training in interventional procedures (preferred)  
Skills 
• Critical care nursing  
• Knowledge of procedures and instruments  
• Emergency response  
• Team coordination  
Work Settings 
• Interventional radiology suites  
• Cath labs  
• Tertiary hospitals  
Salary 
₹4–10 LPA 
Career Growth 
IR Nurse → Senior IR Nurse → Nurse Specialist → Nurse Manager
