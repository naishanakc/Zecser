 RIS Specialist (Radiology Information 
System) 
Job Summary 
A RIS Specialist manages radiology workflow systems, including scheduling, reporting, and 
patient data management. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Maintain RIS software and databases  
• Manage patient scheduling and reporting workflows  
• Ensure smooth integration with PACS  
• Generate operational reports  
• Support hospital staff in system usage  
Qualification 
• BSc in IT / Healthcare Administration / Radiology  
Skills 
• Database management  
• Workflow optimization  
• Software handling  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Healthcare IT companies  
Salary 
₹4–12 LPA 
Career Growth 
RIS Specialist → Healthcare IT Manager → Systems Director
