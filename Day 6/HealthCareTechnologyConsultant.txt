 Healthcare Technology Consultant 
(Radiology Systems) 
Job Summary 
A Healthcare Technology Consultant specializes in advising hospitals on radiology IT 
systems, including PACS, RIS, AI tools, and digital transformation. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Advise on selection and implementation of radiology systems  
• Integrate PACS, RIS, and hospital information systems (HIS)  
• Guide digital transformation initiatives  
• Evaluate and implement AI tools in radiology  
• Ensure system efficiency, security, and compliance  
• Train staff on new technologies  
Qualification 
• BSc/MSc in IT / Radiology / Health Informatics  
• Experience with healthcare IT systems  
• Certifications in healthcare technology (preferred)  
Skills 
• IT systems knowledge (PACS, RIS, HIS)  
• System integration  
• Strategic consulting  
• Communication and client management  
Work Settings 
• IT consulting firms  
• Health-tech companies  
• Hospitals  
• Government healthcare projects  
Salary 
₹10–35+ LPA 
Career Growth 
Tech Consultant → Senior Consultant → Solutions Architect → Healthcare IT Director
