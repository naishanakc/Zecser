Job Summary 
A CT Scan Technician operates Computed Tomography (CT) machines to produce 
detailed cross-sectional images of the body. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Operate CT scanner equipment  
• Administer contrast agents (if required)  
• Monitor patients during scanning  
• Ensure accurate imaging protocols  
• Maintain patient safety and comfort  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology  
• CT specialization training (preferred)  
Skills 
• Knowledge of CT imaging techniques  
• Patient monitoring  
• Attention to detail  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Imaging centers  
Salary 
₹3–7 LPA 
Career Growth 
CT Technician → Senior CT Specialist → Imaging Technologist
