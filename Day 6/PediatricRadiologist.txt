Job Summary 
A Pediatric Radiologist focuses on imaging for infants, children, and adolescents, handling 
congenital and developmental disorders. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Interpret pediatric X-rays, ultrasound, MRI, CT  
• Diagnose congenital abnormalities and childhood diseases  
• Use low-dose radiation protocols for child safety  
• Communicate findings sensitively with parents and pediatricians  
Qualification 
• MBBS + MD/DNB Radiology  
• Fellowship in Pediatric Radiology  
Skills 
• Child-specific anatomy knowledge  
• Patience and communication with families  
• Radiation safety expertise  
Work Settings 
• Children’s hospitals  
• NICU/PICU support units  
Salary 
₹18–50+ LPA
