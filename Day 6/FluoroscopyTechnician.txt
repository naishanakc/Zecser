Job Summary 
A Fluoroscopy Technician operates real-time X-ray imaging systems used to visualize 
internal organs and guide procedures. 
Key Responsibilities 
• Operate fluoroscopy equipment for real-time imaging  
• Assist doctors during procedures (GI studies, angiography, catheter placement)  
• Position patients correctly  
• Ensure radiation protection for patients and staff  
• Maintain imaging equipment and quality  
Qualification 
• Diploma/BSc in Radiology  
• Training in fluoroscopy (preferred)  
Skills 
• Real-time imaging handling  
• Coordination with doctors during procedures  
• Radiation safety awareness  
Work Settings 
• Hospitals  
• Diagnostic centers  
• Surgical units  
Salary 
₹3–7 LPA 
Career Growth 
Fluoroscopy Technician → Advanced Imaging Technologist → Interventional Support
