# AI Hiring Data Entity Design

## Candidate Profile
Stores personal details of the applicant like name, phone, email, and location.

## Skills
List of technologies or abilities the candidate has.  
Can include level and category.

## Experience
Work history including company, designation, duration, and responsibilities.

## Education
Academic qualifications of the candidate.

## Certifications
Professional certificates earned by the candidate.

## Job Description
Information about the job including title, skills required, and experience needed.

## Entity Relationships

- A Candidate can have multiple Skills.
- A Candidate can have multiple Experiences.
- A Candidate can have multiple Education records.
- A Candidate can have multiple Certifications.
- A Job can require multiple Skills.
